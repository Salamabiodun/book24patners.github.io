Giza is famous for its many attractions, if you love antiquities, history and Egypt cultural tours, you will find the Great Pyramids of Giza, you will explore Sakkara, Memphis, and Dahshour. The most important attractions in Giza

Giza Pyramids and the Sphinx

One of the Seven Wonders of the World that puts tourism on a top priority for any tourist looking for looking for Egypt tours or Egypt tour packages.

The Pyramids of Giza, dating back to around 2600 B.C are nine in number including the small pyramids of the princesses. The Sphinx statue is located in the same area of Giza pyramids. The largest statue in ancient Egypt of one single piece of stone.

Saqqara

A great archeological site that contains the step pyramid. It is the oldest Pyramid.The area is rich with many tombs for the nobles and many other pyramids. There is also the Imhotep museum named after the great architect of the step pyramid of Zoser.

Memphis

The first and oldest capital in Ancient Egypt. The city goes back to 3100 B.C when it was built by Narmer

Dahshur

The name of king Snefru is associated with Dahshour. The king had two pyramids built there. The Red Pyramid and the Bent pyramid.

POPULAR QUESTIONS

The best time to visit Egypt is from September until March. It is the perfect time when the weather is good and mild. From September until mid-November, when it is very nice and the temperatures are pleasant for all activities - around 25 C to 15 C. mid-Nov until the end of February is wintertime when temperatures during the day are around 12 or 15 C, and at night can drop to about 7 or 8 C. In March and April, the weather is still so nice and travelers can enjoy the Easter vacation in Egypt.
From May to September is the summer season. Spending summertime in a desert climate does not appeal to most travelers. Average daytime temperatures hover in the high 80s and low to mid-90s, although they can jump up into three digits in the South as in Luxor and Aswan. Summer is really hot here, prices go down and it is a good chance for travelers with a tight budget.

After the danger of the Coronavirus, Egypt is one of the security countries you can visit, the situation is very stable now in Egypt, tourists enjoy tours in Egypt, with safe movement and security by the police in every region of Egypt and all tourist places in particular. Securing tourists in Egypt is the most important priority of the Egyptian government.
