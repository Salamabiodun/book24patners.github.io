Here are the highlights and the most important sites you can visit in Cairo:

The Egyptian Museum

One of the largest museums in the world. It contains about 150 thousand monuments telling the history of Egypt's pharaonic, Roman and Greek history.

The Coptic area

Contains some of the oldest churches in the world like the Hanging church, St Barbara and the holy Cavern church. The area includes the oldest Jewish synagogue in Egypt called Ben Ezra temple.

El-Moez Street

It is one of the best tourism sites in Cairo dating back to the Fatimids and even the Mamluks. It is actually an open-air museum of Islamic structures and architecture. The place was included in the UNESCO World Heritage List in the late 1970s.

Khan El-Khalili

It is one of the biggest in Cairo day tours. The market dates back to 6 centuries and located near Al-Azha square. Through Khan al-Khalili, you can buy some souvenirs that remind you of your Egypt day trips made of different materials. Enjoy the design of the place, which is preserved to this day in its ancient, Fatimid architectural style.

Cairo is the largest city in the Arab world and it has countless attractions such as, a big chain of museums, a big collection of mosques that date back to different eras. Cairo tower, Al Azhar Park, The Opera House, and royal palaces museums are some of the modern attractions in Cairo

POPULAR QUESTIONS

The best time to visit Egypt is from September until March. It is the perfect time when the weather is good and mild. From September until mid-November, when it is very nice and the temperatures are pleasant for all activities - around 25 C to 15 C. mid-Nov until the end of February is wintertime when temperatures during the day are around 12 or 15 C, and at night can drop to about 7 or 8 C. In March and April, the weather is still so nice and travelers can enjoy the Easter vacation in Egypt.
From May to September is the summer season. Spending summertime in a desert climate does not appeal to most travelers. Average daytime temperatures hover in the high 80s and low to mid-90s, although they can jump up into three digits in the South as in Luxor and Aswan. Summer is really hot here, prices go down and it is a good chance for travelers with a tight budget.

After the danger of the Coronavirus, Egypt is one of the security countries you can visit, the situation is very stable now in Egypt, tourists enjoy tours in Egypt, with safe movement and security by the police in every region of Egypt and all tourist places in particular. Securing tourists in Egypt is the most important priority of the Egyptian government.
